Lasagna

A delicious homemade lasagna, perfect for bringing the family together and making the weekend even more special.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasagna noodles
  • 500g of ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 400g of tomato sauce
  • 300g of mozzarella cheese
  • 200g of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of oregano

Preparation Method

  1. In a pan, heat the olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until golden.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until well browned.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and oregano. Cook for 10 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, place a layer of sauce, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles.
  5. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese and another layer of sauce. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  6. Finish with Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (356°F) for 30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
