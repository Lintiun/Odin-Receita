Pasta

A delicious pasta recipe, perfect for a romantic dinner or a family lunch.

Ingredients

  • 250g of pasta of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 400g of peeled tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Preparation Method

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a pan and saut√© the garlic until golden.
  3. Add the peeled tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta and mix it with the sauce.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil on top.

Enjoy your meal!
