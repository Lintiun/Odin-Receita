Pizza

Pizza is a popular dish of Italian origin that consists of a round and flat base of fermented wheat dough, topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, olives, vegetables, meat, etc.), which is baked at high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of wheat flour
  • 1 packet of dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 cup of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 cup of pizza sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Preparation Method

  1. In a bowl, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it rest for 5-10 minutes until it foams.
  2. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until it forms a dough.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a cloth, and let it rise for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat the oven to 245°C (475°F).
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to the desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  8. Spread the pizza sauce over the base, sprinkle the cheese, and add your favorite toppings.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
